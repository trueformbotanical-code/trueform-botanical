Questions

Frequently asked

Is herbal consultation safe alongside conventional medicine?▾
Yes — all our herbalists are trained to screen for herb-drug interactions and will always review your current medications. They complement, never replace, your medical team.
How does the consultation process work?▾
You start by registering a free account and submitting your health assessment. Your assigned herbalist reviews it, schedules a video or phone consultation, then sends your personalised herbal protocol. You can message your herbalist anytime through the portal.
How is my personal health data protected?▾
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit via Supabase. We operate HIPAA-aligned infrastructure with role-based access controls. We never sell your data and you may request full deletion at any time.
Can I access consultations from anywhere in the world?▾
Absolutely. We serve clients in 48+ countries. Video sessions work on any device with flexible timezone scheduling.
What is included in the client portal?▾
Your portal includes your health assessment, personalised herbal protocol, a daily symptom diary to track your progress, direct messaging with your practitioner, and appointment scheduling — all in one secure place.
Do you offer a free initial consultation?▾
Yes. We offer a free 15-minute introductory call for all new clients. Register through the portal and our team will be in touch to schedule your complimentary call.
