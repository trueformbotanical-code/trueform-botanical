Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about herbal consultations, your portal, pricing, and how we keep your data safe.

Consultations
How does the consultation process work?▾
Register a free account and submit your health assessment. Your assigned herbalist reviews it, schedules a video or phone consultation, then sends your personalised herbal protocol. You can message your herbalist anytime through the portal.
How long does an initial consultation take?▾
Initial consultations are 60–90 minutes, giving your herbalist time to fully understand your health history, lifestyle, and goals. Follow-up sessions are typically 30–45 minutes.
Can I access consultations from anywhere in the world?▾
Absolutely. We serve clients in 48+ countries. All consultations are conducted via video or phone call with flexible timezone scheduling, 7 days a week.
What traditions do your herbalists practice?▾
Our practitioners are trained across four major traditions: Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), Western Phytotherapy, and African Botanical Medicine. When you register, you can indicate which tradition resonates with you, or we will match you with the best fit.
Do you offer a free initial consultation?▾
Yes. We offer a free 15-minute introductory call for all new clients. Register through the portal and our team will be in touch to schedule your complimentary call within 24 hours.
What happens after my consultation?▾
Your herbalist will prepare a personalised herbal protocol — a detailed plan including specific herbs, dosages, preparation methods, and lifestyle recommendations. This is sent to you via your Client Portal within 48 hours of your consultation. Follow-up check-ins are scheduled at 4–6 week intervals.
Client Portal
What is included in the Client Portal?▾
Your portal includes your health assessment, personalised herbal protocol, a daily symptom diary, direct messaging with your practitioner, appointment scheduling, and your full consultation history — all in one secure place.
Can I edit my health assessment after submitting it?▾
Yes — you can edit or delete your assessment at any time before your herbalist begins their review. Once your assessment is under review or you have been assigned to a herbalist, it becomes view-only to protect the integrity of your consultation.
How do I message my herbalist?▾
Log into your Client Portal and use the Messages section on your dashboard. Your herbalist will be notified and typically responds within one business day.
What is the daily symptom diary?▾
The symptom diary lets you log how you are feeling each day — mood, energy, sleep quality, pain levels, and any relevant notes. This data helps your herbalist track your progress and adjust your protocol over time.
Is there a mobile app?▾
The portal is fully mobile-optimised and works on all smartphones and tablets through your browser — no app download required. For the best experience on iOS, you can add it to your home screen via Safari's Share → Add to Home Screen option.
Pricing
How much does a consultation cost?▾
Initial consultations start from $85. Follow-up sessions are from $55. Your first 15-minute introductory call is always free. All prices are in US dollars with no hidden fees.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfer. Payment is processed securely — we never store your card details.
Is there a cancellation policy?▾
You may cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your session at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment are charged 50% of the session fee. No-shows are charged in full.
Do you offer packages or subscription plans?▾
Yes. We offer a 3-month wellness package that includes an initial consultation, two follow-ups, protocol preparation, and unlimited portal messaging — starting at $220. Ask your herbalist about the right plan for your goals.
Safety & Privacy
Is herbal consultation safe alongside conventional medicine?▾
Yes — all our herbalists are trained to screen for herb-drug interactions and will always review your current medications before making any recommendations. Herbal medicine complements, but never replaces, your medical team.
How is my personal health data protected?▾
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We operate HIPAA-aligned infrastructure with role-based access controls — your data is only visible to you and your assigned herbalist. We never sell your data and you may request full deletion at any time.
Are herbal protocols safe during pregnancy?▾
Some herbs are contraindicated during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Always inform your herbalist if you are pregnant, trying to conceive, or nursing. We have practitioners who specialise in prenatal and postnatal herbal care and can recommend safe, evidence-based options.
Do your herbalists hold recognised qualifications?▾
Yes. All practitioners are certified by nationally or internationally recognised herbal medicine bodies, hold professional liability insurance, and complete ongoing professional development. Their credentials are reviewed annually by our clinical director.
